# Reference files

This directory keeps a small local mirror of open reference material used by
the README. Publisher pages and DOI links remain the canonical bibliography;
local PDFs are included only when there is a direct open source.

## Local open files

- `park-woodburn-1994-suslin-stability.pdf`: arXiv PDF for H. Park and
  C. Woodburn, "An algorithmic proof of Suslin's stability theorem over
  polynomial rings." The arXiv TeX source is also kept as
  `arXiv-alg-geom9405003v1`.
- `barwick-stone-2011-quillensuslin-macaulay2.pdf`: arXiv PDF for Brett
  Barwick and Branden Stone, "The QuillenSuslin Package for Macaulay2."

## Not mirrored locally

The older Journal of Algebra, Mathematische Nachrichten, Computational
Complexity, Springer, Wiley, and IEEE references are linked from `README.md`
through DOI or project pages. They are not mirrored here unless an open
preprint or author-hosted PDF is located.
